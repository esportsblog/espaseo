What is the Biography of Actor Lucas Black?

Actor Lucas Black was born on November 29, 1982, in Speake, Alabama. Growing up in a close-knit family, he was encouraged to pursue his interests in acting from a young age. He attended the University of Southern California, where he honed his skills and laid the foundation for his future career. Lucas made his film debut in the 2003 movie "The War at Home," which garnered him recognition and opened the door to numerous opportunities in the industry.
