What is Ethel Kennedy's Biography?

Ethel Kennedy was born on April 11, 1928, in Chicago, Illinois. She grew up in a well-to-do family, the daughter of a successful businessman and a socialite. After attending several prestigious schools, Ethel went on to graduate from the University of Vermont. Her life took a significant turn when she met Robert F. Kennedy in 1945, whom she married in 1950. Together, they had eleven children and became a prominent couple in American politics and culture.
