Who is Ethel Kennedy? A Brief Biography

Ethel Kennedy, born on April 11, 1928, in Chicago, Illinois, is the widow of Robert F. Kennedy, a U.S. Senator and a prominent figure in the civil rights movement. Ethel was raised in a well-to-do family and became a passionate advocate for social justice and community health issues throughout her life. Her marriage to Robert F. Kennedy in 1950 marked the beginning of a shared journey dedicated to public service and advocacy.
