Who is Bruno Mars?

Bruno Mars, born Peter Gene Hernandez on October 8, 1985, in Honolulu, Hawaii, is a singer, songwriter, record producer, and dancer. He gained fame for his unique blend of pop, R&B, reggae, and funk music.
