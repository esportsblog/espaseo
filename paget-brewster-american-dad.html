What is Paget Brewster's Biography?

Paget Brewster was born on March 10, 1970, in Concord, Massachusetts. She developed an interest in acting during her school years, eventually moving to New York City to pursue a career in theatre and television. Brewster's breakout role came as a recurring character on the hit show "Friends," where she played Kathy, showcasing her comedic talent and charm. She later gained recognition for her work on "Criminal Minds" as Emily Prentiss, further solidifying her status as a talented actress.
