What is Tom Hughes' Biography?

Tom Hughes was born on April 18, 1986, in Upton, Cheshire, England. He attended the Royal Welsh College of Music & Drama, which laid the foundation for his acting career. Hughes made his television debut in the series “The Story of Tracy Beaker” in 2002 and gained recognition for his role in “Silk,” where he played the ambitious barrister, Junior, alongside Maxine Peake. His film credits include roles in “The Last Witness,” “Cemetery Junction,” and the critically acclaimed “Victoria,” where he portrayed Prince Albert.
