Who is Kristi Noem?

Kristi Noem is an American politician, entrepreneur, and mother who has served as the Governor of South Dakota since January 2019. Before her gubernatorial position, she served as a U.S. Representative for South Dakota from 2011 to 2019. Noem is known for her strong conservative views and has been a vocal advocate for various policies that promote economic growth, education, and public safety.
