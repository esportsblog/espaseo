What is Nelly Furtado's Biography?

Nelly Furtado, born on December 2, 1978, in Victoria, British Columbia, Canada, is a renowned singer-songwriter and record producer. She gained widespread recognition after the release of her debut album, "Whoa, Nelly!" in 2000, which included hits like "I'm Like a Bird" and "Turn Off the Light." Furtado's music style is eclectic, incorporating elements of pop, hip-hop, R&B, and folk.
